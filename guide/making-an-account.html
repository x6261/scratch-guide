Making an Account

Go to the Scratch website

First click this link to reach the Scratch website, where we will be making all of our projects: Scratch

Once you’re on the main Scratch homepage, click the “Join Scratch” button in the top-right corner. This will take you to the sign-up screen shown below.

Continue following the on-screen instructions.

You’ll be asked for details like your username, password, country, age, and gender. After that, complete the email verification. This may require a parent or guardian’s email if you’re under a certain age. Once you’ve verified your email, your Scratch account is ready to go!
